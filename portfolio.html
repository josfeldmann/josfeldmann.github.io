About Me

Hi! I'm Joey, a game programmer and designer from Silver Spring, Maryland. I started making Unity games in college and published my first video game PictoPull in 2023. I graduated from University of Maryland College Park with a degree in Computer Science, and have worked 3+ years as a professional software developer.

I got into game dev by implementing different features/systems from games I love in Unity and trying to make a game out of them. I started by trying to make a Fire Emblem style game. My first published game ended up being PictoPull, which is now open source, and heavily based on the Pushmo series. I am currently working on a VR game and a monster catching RPG framework, which I plan to open source (after I finish a game or two with it).
