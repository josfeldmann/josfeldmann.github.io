About Me

Hi! I'm Joseph Feldmann, an Indie Unity Developer. I recently published my first Indie game PictoPull and am looking for new projects to work on. I have been making games for 5+ years now, mostly in Unity. I developed PictoPull part time for 1.5 years while I worked as a software developer and after its release I decided to take a break from software development and do indie dev full time.
